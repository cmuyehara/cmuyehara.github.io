Hi there!
I'm a graduate student in the McKay Lab at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I study the epigenetics of gene regulation during Drosophila development using a mixture of wet-lab and computational techniques. My project has focused on understanding how the steroid hormone ecdysone can induce temporal- and tissue-specific gene expression responses.
